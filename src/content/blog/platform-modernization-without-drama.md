Platform engineering only works when it is tied to the operating model of the organization. A platform team that is disconnected from developer friction, delivery constraints, or governance realities usually creates another layer to navigate instead of leverage.

A better pattern is to treat the platform as a force multiplier for the teams shipping products:

1. Reduce repeated operational work.
2. Standardize the parts that are genuinely common.
3. Preserve clear ownership at the edges.
4. Measure success in delivery outcomes, not platform feature count.

That framing keeps platform work practical and far more likely to survive contact with enterprise reality.
