When you explain a technical position publicly, weak assumptions surface quickly. Talks compress ideas, expose vague reasoning, and force a more defensible view of the tradeoffs.

That is one reason speaking is not separate from architecture work for me. It is part of how I test it, refine it, and make it useful to other people.
