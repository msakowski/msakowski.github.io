The most useful cloud transformation work is rarely the most visually impressive. It is the work that gives teams a credible path from ambition to implementation.

That usually means asking harder questions earlier:

- Which platform capabilities need to exist first?
- Which ownership boundaries are helping or hurting change?
- What operating burden are we introducing by design?

Architecture becomes much more valuable when it reduces execution ambiguity, not when it simply produces a cleaner slide.
