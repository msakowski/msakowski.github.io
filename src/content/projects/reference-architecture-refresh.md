This work focused on helping enterprise retail organizations turn cloud ambition into implementable platform and application decisions.

The main work involved:

- clarifying target states for platform and product teams
- reducing unnecessary architectural complexity
- aligning modernization work with ownership and execution realities

The result was architecture that teams could use to sequence real work instead of producing another strategy artifact that stalled in review.
